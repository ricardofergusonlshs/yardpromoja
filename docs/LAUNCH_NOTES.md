# YardPromoJa Launch Notes

## Positioning

YardPromoJa is Jamaica’s promo and link-up planning platform.

Core message:

```text
Social media gets attention. YardPromoJa turns it into action.
```

## What the site now supports

- Dark premium YardPromoJa homepage.
- Plan the Link-Up page.
- Browse dark promo discovery.
- Campaigns & giveaways.
- Ad detail page polish.
- Share Pack.
- WhatsApp/Facebook/X/TikTok sharing.
- Flyer download.
- Open Graph/social thumbnails.
- Mobile-friendly navigation.
- SEO/PWA routes.
- Health checks.
- Dashboard/admin styling support.

```text
YardPromoJa is live — turn your flyer, event, sale, or campaign into a clean promo page people can view, share, save, and act on.

Post your promo. Share your link. Plan the full link-up.
```

```text
Don’t just post a flyer. Give your flyer a home.

Your Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and WhatsApp posts bring attention. YardPromoJa gives people one clean link where they can view the details, contact you, RSVP, get directions, share the flyer, save the date, and discover related offers.
```
